Buying

Purchasing from us

01

How do I purchase a card?

Purchases are made by reaching out to us directly through email or social media. Send us a screenshot or description of the item you are interested in, and we’ll help confirm availability and next steps.

02

Are the cards on the inventory page actually available?

Yes. Cards shown on the inventory page are available for purchase unless they were recently claimed and the page has not updated yet.

03

Are the inventory images real photos?

Yes. The images shown on the inventory page are exact scans of the real card or item being sold.

04

Can I request better images?

Yes. You can contact us to request additional photos, closeups, or condition details for any product. We are very responsive on social media.

Trade-Ins

Selling cards to us

07

Do you buy cards from customers?

Yes. We review singles, graded cards, and sealed products through our trade-in form. You can choose whether you prefer cash or store credit.

08

Do you accept bulk?

No. We do not accept bulk. Items should generally be worth $5 or more each.

09

Should I ship cards right after submitting the trade-in form?

No. Please do not ship anything until your submission has been reviewed and we contact you with next steps.

10

What are typical trade-in rates?

Cash offers are typically around 70%, and store credit is typically around 80%. Some items may receive higher offers depending on the item, condition, demand, and market value.
