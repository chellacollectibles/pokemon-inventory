How It Works

Simple process

01

Submit the form

Fill out the trade-in form with your name, email, item type, and a short description of what you want to send.

02

We review it

We review your submission and follow up by email with details, questions, or approval before any shipping happens.

03

Ship after approval

If approved, you’ll receive next steps. Items should be packaged properly and shipped safely to help avoid damage in transit.

04

Receive payment

Once the package is received and processed, payment is generally sent within 1 week depending on the trade details.
